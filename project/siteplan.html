Animal Shelter Website Site Plan

1. Site Name

Chosen Name: Paws & Hearts Animal Shelter

Description: The name “Paws & Hearts” reflects compassion, care, and the connection between people and animals. It’s memorable, warm, and suitable for a shelter focused on animal adoption and rescue.

Optional Domain: pawsandhearts.org

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help connect loving people with animals in need of adoption. It will provide information about available pets, shelter services, volunteer opportunities, and donation options. Visitors can learn about the shelter’s mission and how they can get involved.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I adopt a pet and what is the adoption process?
  • Where can I find information about volunteering or donating to the shelter?
  • Are there any special events or adoption drives coming up soon?

4. Color Schema

The website will use a warm, friendly color palette to represent care and trust.

  • Primary Color: #F4A261 (used for buttons, headers, and highlights)
  • Secondary Color: #2A9D8F (used for backgrounds and accents)

5. Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – used for titles and section headers.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and general text.
