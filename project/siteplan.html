Animal Shelter Website Planning Document

Fur Ever Home

Chosen Name: Fur Ever Home

Description: Fur Ever Home reflects compassion, care, and the connection between people and animals. It is memorable, warm, and suitable for a shelter focused on animal adoption and rescue.

Domain: fureverhome.org

For the Fur Ever Home Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help connect loving people with animals in need of adoption. It will provide information about available pets, shelter services, volunteer opportunities, and donation options. Visitors can learn about the shelters mission and how they can get involved.

For the Fur Ever Home Scenarios

  • How can I adopt a pet and what is the adoption process?
  • Where can I find information about volunteering or donating to the shelter?
  • Are there any special events or adoption drives coming up soon?

For the Fur Ever Home Color Schema

The website will use a warm, friendly color palette to represent care and trust.

  • Primary Color: #F4A261 (used for buttons, headers, and highlights)
  • Secondary Color: #2A9D8F (used for backgrounds and accents)

For the Fur Ever Home Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif, used for titles and section headers.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif, used for paragraphs and general text.
