For the Fur Ever Home Website

Fur Ever Home reflects a connection between people and animals. It is a shelter focused on animal adoption and rescue.

For the Fur Ever Home Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help connect people with animals in need of adoption. It will provide information about available pets, volunteer opportunities, and donation options.

For the Fur Ever Home Scenarios

How can I adopt a pet and what is the adoption process?

Where can I find information about volunteering or donating to the shelter?

For the Fur Ever Home Color Schema

The website will use a warm, friendly color palette.

Primary Color: #11695f (used for buttons, headers, and highlights)

Secondary Color: #f3e1d2 (used for backgrounds and accents)

For the Fur Ever Home Typography

Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif, (used for titles and section headers)

Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif, (used for paragraphs and general text)
